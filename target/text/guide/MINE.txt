~Raw Material Name~
A matéria prima que está sendo produzida.^
~Raw Material Stock~
This area shows the stock of the raw material in the unit. Each box represents one cargo of the raw material. Each cargo can store a certain quantity of the raw material.^
~Utilization~
A barra marrom mostra a utilização de cada unidade. If this bar reaches its full length, the unit has reached its full capacity.
